James is a self-taught photographer currently based in Chicago, IL.

His passion for photography grew when he started travelling in 2012 and has been perfecting his craft since. Although his specialties lie in landscape and portrait photography, he has ventured into many types of photography genres in pursuit of fullfilling his creativity.

His other interests include studies of color management system, print technologies, and badminton. Futhermore, James is a software engineer by day and print technology consultant by night.
